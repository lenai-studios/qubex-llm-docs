# Private managed Qubex LLM

Qubex LLM cloud is the easiest way to trial and scale Qubex LLM for your business or personal use.

The Mintplex Labs team offers **isolated** and **private** instances of Qubex LLM that you can use to try out or scale up Qubex LLM for your business or personal use.

Each instance is hosted on an isolated AWS instance that is automatically updated and managed by the Mintplex Labs core team. Your data and resources are not shared with any other customers who are using our managed service.
